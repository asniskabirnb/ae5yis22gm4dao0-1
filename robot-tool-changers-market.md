<p><p>The Robot Tool Changers market is divided into two primary types: manual and automatic. Manual robot tool changers require human intervention to switch tools, offering simplicity and lower costs, making them suitable for tasks with less frequent tool changes. In contrast, automatic robot tool changers enable robots to autonomously switch tools, increasing efficiency and productivity in fast-paced environments. These automatic systems are ideal for applications requiring versatile task execution, such as assembly lines and complex manufacturing processes.</p></p>
