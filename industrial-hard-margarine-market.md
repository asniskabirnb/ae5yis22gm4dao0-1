<p><p>The industrial hard margarine market encompasses three main types: spreadable, all-purpose, and butter blend. Spreadable margarine is designed for easy application, remaining soft and creamy even at refrigeration temperatures, making it ideal for sandwiches and cooking. All-purpose margarine serves as a versatile cooking ingredient, suitable for baking and frying, offering a neutral flavor. Butter blend margarine combines the richness of butter with margarine, providing a buttery flavor while enhancing spreadability and cooking qualities, appealing to consumers seeking convenience and taste.</p></p>
