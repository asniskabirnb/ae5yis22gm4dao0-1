<p><strong>The Flash Point Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cup Flash Point Tester</li><li>Closed Cup Flash Point Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flash Point Tester market is primarily divided into two types: Open Cup Flash Point Testers and Closed Cup Flash Point Testers. Open Cup testers measure the flash point of substances by exposing them to an open environment, making them suitable for higher boiling point liquids. In contrast, Closed Cup testers confine the sample in a sealed environment, providing more accurate results for volatile substances at lower temperatures. Both types are essential for assessing the flammability characteristics of various materials in safety and regulatory contexts.</p></p>
